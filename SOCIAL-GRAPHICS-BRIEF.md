# Social Graphics Brief for PsalMix Waitlist Launch

**Brand Overview:**
- **Name:** PsalMix
- **Tagline:** "Music Your Family Can Trust"
- **Mission:** Family-safe music streaming where every song is parent-vetted
- **Vibe:** Warm, trustworthy, modern, joyful (not preachy or restrictive)

---

## Brand Colors

**Primary:**
- **Purple:** #8B4BCF (bright, uplifting, approachable)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (clean, spacious)

**Secondary (optional accents):**
- **Cream:** #F9F7F4 (warm background alternative)
- **Soft Coral:** #FF9E80 (energetic accent)
- **Light Sage:** #A8D5BA (calming accent)

**Text:**
- **Dark Charcoal:** #2D2D2D (readable, not harsh black)
- **Medium Gray:** #6B6B6B (secondary text)

---

## Typography

**Headers:**
- Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold, or Avenir Heavy)
- Large, confident, easy to read on mobile

**Body Text:**
- Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Lato, or system font)
- High contrast with background for readability

**Logo/Brand Name:**
- Custom or standout font (slightly playful but still trustworthy)
- Consider musical note integrated into logo (🎵)

---

## Image Style

**Photography:**
- Real families (not overly staged stock photos)
- Diverse representation (various ages, ethnicities, family structures)
- Joyful moments: dancing in kitchen, car rides, kids with headphones, family gatherings
- Natural lighting, warm tones

**Illustrations (if used):**
- Simple, modern line art
- Friendly, approachable (not childish)
- Music-themed icons (headphones, musical notes, playlists, etc.)

**Avoid:**
- Overly religious imagery (cross, church, etc.) — keep inclusive
- Sterile/corporate stock photos
- Dark or moody aesthetics (stay uplifting!)

## Usage Guidelines

**Text Overlays:**
- Keep copy SHORT (5-10 words max for social posts)
- High contrast (dark text on light, or vice versa)
- Avoid center-alignment for long text (left-align for readability)

**Imagery:**
- Always include faces (human connection drives engagement)
- Smile and eye contact (builds trust)
- Show diversity (not just one family type)

**Call-to-Action:**
- Always include CTA ("Join the Waitlist," "Link in Bio," "Swipe Up")
- Make it visually distinct (button, arrow, bright color)

**Branding:**
- Logo in corner or bottom (not overpowering)
- Consistent color palette across all graphics
- Recognizable style (people should know it's PsalMix at a glance)

## A/B Testing Ideas

Test variations to see what performs best:

**Version A:** Photo-heavy (family images, real moments)  
**Version B:** Text-heavy (bold statements, minimal design)

**Version A:** Purple background (#8B4BCF)  
**Version B:** Cream/white background (#F9F7F4)

**Version A:** Founder story angle (McKinzie's photo + quote)  
**Version B:** Problem/solution angle (pain point → PsalMix)

Track engagement (likes, comments, shares, click-throughs) and double down on what works!
