## Contingency Plans

### If Form Breaks
1. **Immediate:** Post comment/story: "We're experiencing high traffic! If form isn't loading, DM me your email and I'll add you manually."
2. **Backup:** Create Google Form as temporary alternative
3. **Fix:** Contact developer/tech support immediately

### If Engagement Is Low (< 50 signups by noon)
1. **Post IG Story poll:** "Would you use a family-safe music app? Yes/No" (engagement boost)
2. **Go live on Instagram:** Talk about why you built it (authentic, unscripted)
3. **DM 20 friends directly:** "Hey, could you help me share this?"

### If You Get Negative Comments
**Response Templates:**

**"This is unnecessary. Just monitor what your kids listen to."**
> "I hear you! For some families, manual monitoring works great. For me with 3 kids and homeschooling, I wanted a tool that gave me peace of mind without the extra work. PsalMix is just another option for families who want it. 😊"

**"Why not just use [other app]?"**
> "Great question! [Other app] is awesome for some families. PsalMix is specifically built for parents who want every song vetted by real parents, not just algorithms. Different tools for different needs!"

**"You're being too overprotective."**
> "Every family has different values, and that's okay! PsalMix is for families who specifically want this level of curation. No judgment either way. 💜"

### If Overwhelmed by DMs
1. **Use quick replies:** Set up Instagram quick replies for common questions
2. **Post FAQ Story:** Answer top 5 questions in Stories to reduce repeat DMs
3. **Batch responses:** Respond every 30-60 minutes instead of instantly
