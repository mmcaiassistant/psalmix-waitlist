## Email Design Tips

**Keep It Simple:**
- Plain text or minimal design (feels more personal)
- Single CTA button (don't overwhelm with multiple links)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Mobile-friendly (60% of opens will be on mobile)

**Key Elements:**
1. **Hero image** (optional): Simple graphic with PsalMix logo or family photo
2. **CTA button**: Purple (#8B4BCF), clear text ("Join the Waitlist")
3. **Footer**: Unsubscribe link, contact info, social links

**Tone:**
- Warm, personal (like an email from a friend)
- Authentic, not salesy
- Hopeful, not preachy

## Metrics to Track

**Day 1:**
- Open rate (goal: 30-40%)
- Click-through rate (goal: 10-15%)
- Unsubscribes (expect 1-2%)

**Day 7:**
- Total waitlist signups from email
- Forward rate (if trackable)
- Replies (read and respond to every one!)

**Adjust for next send:**
- If open rate low → test different subject lines
- If CTR low → simplify email, make CTA more prominent
- If unsubscribes high → tone down frequency or adjust positioning
