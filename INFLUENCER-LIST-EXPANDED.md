## NOTES FOR RECRUITMENT STRATEGY

1. **Email-First Approach:** Many influencers have public email in bio—prioritize direct email outreach for professionalism
2. **DM Secondary:** Use DM as follow-up after email or for immediate contact details
3. **Values Messaging:** Lead with shared values (faith, family protection, intentional parenting) not follower count
4. **Micro-Influencer Advantage:** These micro-influencers typically have:
   - Higher engagement rates than macro-influencers
   - More responsive to partnership opportunities
   - Strong, dedicated community bonds
   - Better ROI for startup partnerships
5. **Cross-Platform Reach:** Many have Instagram + TikTok + blogs—multi-touch strategy recommended
6. **Community Size Benefits:** Combined reach of 25-30 micro-influencers (1.5M-2M total) can rival single macro-influencer with higher engagement
