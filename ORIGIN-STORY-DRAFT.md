## The Medium Version (About Page / Email Signature)

I'm McKinzie — a mom of three, homeschooler, and lifelong music lover.

I grew up with music playing constantly. Road trips, kitchen dance parties, homework sessions — music was the soundtrack of my childhood. I wanted the same for my kids.

But somewhere along the way, music got complicated.

Every playlist became a minefield. Every new song required previewing. Every car ride meant my finger hovering over the skip button, ready to lunge before my 7-year-old heard something I'd have to explain.

I tried the "clean" filters. They don't work. They catch the F-word but miss everything else — the sexual themes, the drug glorification, the darkness dressed up in a catchy beat.

So I built PsalMix.

Not a Christian-only app. Not a kids-only app. A real music streaming service with every genre — pop, country, hip-hop, rock — where every single song is verified safe by actual humans before it ever reaches your family's ears.

Now I hand my kids the phone and actually enjoy the music WITH them.

That's what I want for your family too.

### The Failed Solutions

Like every parent, I tried the obvious solutions first.

**The "clean" filter on Spotify?** Turns out it only catches songs that are *labeled* explicit. It doesn't catch songs that are *about* explicit things. Big difference.

**Christian radio?** Great for worship music, but my kids wanted pop and hip-hop and country — not just Sunday morning playlists.

**Previewing every song?** Exhausting. And impossible when your kids are old enough to add their own songs.

**Just giving up?** Not an option. I believe what we put in our minds matters.

### Why I Built PsalMix

I'm a mom of three. I homeschool. I'm a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

But more than any of that — I'm a music lover.

I didn't want to raise my kids in silence or fear. I wanted them to discover the joy of music the way I did — freely, joyfully, without constant monitoring.

I just wanted to know that when they pressed play, whatever came through those speakers would be something I could trust.

So I built it.

PsalMix isn't "kids music" — it's real music. Pop hits, country anthems, hip-hop bangers, rock classics. Every genre. Every era.

The difference? Before any song enters our library, a real human reviews it. Not an algorithm. Not a label's "explicit" tag. An actual person who asks: "Would I be comfortable if my 8-year-old heard this?"

If the answer is no — it doesn't get in.

### What PsalMix Is (And Isn't)

**PsalMix IS:**
- Family-safe music streaming
- All genres — pop, hip-hop, country, rock, R&B
- Clean versions of mainstream hits
- Human-curated (not algorithm-filtered)
- A family plan you can trust

**PsalMix ISN'T:**
- Christian-only music
- Kids-only music
- Censored/bleeped tracks (we just don't include problem songs)
- Another app that promises "clean" but doesn't deliver
