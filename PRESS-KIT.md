## The Problem We Solve

**50% of Spotify's top-streamed songs weekly contain explicit content.**

But here's what parents don't know: the "explicit filter" only catches obvious profanity (the F-word, etc.). It does NOT filter:
- Sexual themes and innuendo
- Drug glorification
- Violence and dark imagery
- Suggestive album art
- Podcasts with adult content

Parents think their kids are safe. They're not.

**PsalMix is the solution:** Every song is reviewed by our editorial team before it enters our library. No algorithms. No automation. Just real people making sure your family can press play without worry.

## Founder Bio

**McKinzie McCall** is a Utah-based entrepreneur, mother of three, and the founder of PsalMix.

After years of frustration with mainstream music streaming—constantly hovering over the skip button, previewing songs before road trips, and dealing with the inevitable "Mom, what does that mean?"—McKinzie decided to build the solution herself.

"I realized that 'clean' filters don't actually work," says McCall. "They catch the F-word but miss everything else. I wanted a service where I could hand my kids the phone and actually trust what they'd hear."

McKinzie is also the founder of multiple successful content properties and brings deep expertise in building products that serve family-focused audiences.
