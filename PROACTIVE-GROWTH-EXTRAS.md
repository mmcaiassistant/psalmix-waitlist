## 🔥 VIRAL CONTENT IDEAS

### TikTok/Reels Concepts

1. **"POV: Spotify's 'clean' filter"**  
   Show parent's face as inappropriate lyrics play from "clean" playlist
   
2. **"Tell me you're a PsalMix family without telling me"**  
   Kids dancing to music, parents NOT hovering over skip button

3. **"Things I don't have to do anymore"**  
   List: Preview every song, hover over skip, explain awkward lyrics

4. **"The Spotify test"**  
   Play top 10 songs on "clean" mode, count inappropriate themes (spoiler: most have them)

5. **"My kid asked me what [lyric] means"**  
   Awkward parent face, then show PsalMix as solution

## 🎁 SURPRISE EXTRAS FOR MCKENZIE

### 1. Competitor Weakness Exploit
**Gabb Music's #1 complaint:** "No family plan pricing — gets expensive with multiple kids"
**PsalMix advantage:** Family plan from day 1. Hit this HARD in messaging.

### 2. Viral Hook Idea
**"The $7 Test"** — Challenge parents to play Spotify's "clean" playlist for 7 songs and count inappropriate themes. Post results. Tag PsalMix.

### 3. Launch Timing
**Best days to launch waitlist push:** Tuesday-Thursday (highest email open rates, social engagement)
**Best time:** 10am-12pm EST (moms checking phones during kids' activities)

### 4. Hidden Audience
**Youth group leaders** — They need safe music for events, car rides, activities. Target them specifically.

### 5. Referral Hack
**"Refer your whole small group"** — Create messaging for church small groups to join together. Group referral codes.
