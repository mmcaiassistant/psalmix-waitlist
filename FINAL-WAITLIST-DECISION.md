### How It Works

1. **Sign up** → Get queue position (#1,247)
2. **Share your link** → Friends sign up, you move up
3. **Hit milestones** → Unlock rewards
4. **Launch day** → Queue position determines access order
5. **Subscribe** → Your rewards activate (50% off, free months, etc.)

**Key point:** Rewards are earned on the waitlist but only realized when they become paying customers.
