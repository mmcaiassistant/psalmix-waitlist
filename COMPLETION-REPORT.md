**Tagline:** "Stream Hits. Not Regrets."

## 💡 Key Insights That Will Make or Break This

1. **Price at $9.99, NOT $7.99 or $12.99**
   - $7.99 signals "cheap/inferior" vs. Gabb's $10.99
   - $12.99 too close to mainstream individual plans
   - $9.99 = sweet spot (proven, accessible, premium enough)

2. **Founding Member Offer MUST be "For Life"**
   - Creates urgency (lock in forever vs. "first year")
   - Builds loyal base (zero churn when price locked)
   - Word-of-mouth > revenue sacrifice

3. **Don't Position as "Christian Music Streaming"**
   - The Overflow tried this and FAILED (2015-2020)
   - Safety-conscious parents (religious + secular) = 10x bigger market
   - Lead with SAFETY, not faith

4. **Two-Sided Referral Incentives Are Critical**
   - Friend MUST get value (25 spots + founding eligibility)
   - Both sides win = 2-3x more referrals
   - Target viral coefficient 1.5+ = exponential growth

5. **Target Gabb Music Users First**
   - They're already paying $10.99/device
   - PsalMix is cheaper ($9.99 family) + better (parental dashboard)
   - Retargeting ads to Gabb Facebook followers = high-intent leads

## ⚠️ Potential Risks & Mitigation

**Risk: Referral fraud (fake signups)**
- Mitigation: Email verification required, IP tracking, manual review for 20+ referrals

**Risk: Gabb Music adds family plan before we launch**
- Mitigation: We still have parental dashboard, $6.99 founding rate, better UX

**Risk: Mainstream services improve parental controls**
- Mitigation: Human curation > algorithms (our differentiator), faster to market

**Risk: Waitlist fatigue (people forget by launch)**
- Mitigation: Engagement emails, milestone updates, urgency campaign

**Risk: 40% conversion assumption too optimistic**
- Mitigation: Conservative estimate (Dropbox hit 35%), founding rate drives urgency
